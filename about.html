Our Journey

From late-night dreams to your daily hangout

2020

The Pop-Up Era

Armed with a blender, a Bluetooth speaker, and way too much enthusiasm, we launched our first booth at a local fan convention. Sold out of purple boba in two hours. Knew we were onto something.

2021

Building the Fandom

Our Instagram blew up. Fans started DMing us asking when we'd open a real cafe. We hosted underground streaming parties in rented spaces. The demand was undeniable.

2022

We signed the lease on our first permanent space. Fans helped us paint the walls, hang the albums, and set up the lightstick display. Opening day felt like a concertâ€”tears, cheers, and all.
